Complete Guide to UAE Golden Visa in 2026

GW

GlobalWays Editorial

UAE Services Team

The UAE Golden Visa offers 5- or 10-year residency to investors, entrepreneurs, specialized talents, and outstanding students. Here's everything you need to know in 2026.

Eligibility Routes

There are several pathways to qualify: property investment (AED 2M+), business investment, exceptional talent in science/arts/sports, and specialized professionals in priority sectors like healthcare and engineering.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of investment or qualification
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Emirates ID application
